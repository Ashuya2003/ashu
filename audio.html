SILAPPATHIKARAM
Ilango Adigal
Silappathikaram is an ancient Tamil epic written by Ilango Adigal in the 2nd century CE. The story revolves around Kovalan and Kannagi, a married couple from Puhar. Kovalan falls in love with a courtesan named Madhavi, leading to financial ruin and his wrongful execution by the Pandya king. Kannagi seeks justice, proves Kovalan's innocence, and in her fury, destroys the city of Madurai. The epic explores themes of love, justice, and the consequences of corruption, showcasing the moral and ethical values of ancient Tamil society.
THOUGHT VIBRATION
william walker atkinson
"Vibration Thoughts" is a concept associated with the idea that our thoughts and emotions emit energy or vibrations that can impact our experiences and the world around us. It suggests that positive and negative thoughts can attract similar energies, influencing one's life and well-being.
THINK LIKE A MONK
Jayshetty
"Think Like a Monk" offers transformative insights from Jay Shetty's monastic experiences, guiding readers to discover purpose and mindfulness for a more fulfilling life. The book blends ancient wisdom with practical advice, inspiring a monk-inspired mindset in everyday living.
MEGA LIVING
Robin Sharma
"Megaliving" by Robin Sharma is a motivational book that provides practical strategies for achieving success and living a fulfilling life. Sharma shares insights on personal development, productivity, and adopting positive habits, encouraging readers to lead a life of purpose and excellence.
IKIGAI
Héctor García and Francesc Miralles
"Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life" explores the concept of Ikigai, the intersection of what you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for. The book delves into the principles of finding purpose, fulfillment, and longevity by aligning one's passions with meaningful contributions to the world.
THE SEVEN CHAKRAS
The seven chakras represent energy centers in the body, influencing physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects. Balancing these chakras promotes holistic well-being and spiritual harmony.
